# 📊 Data Story: E-Commerce Customer Behavior

## 🎯 Objective
To understand customer behavior, spending patterns, and product preferences.

---

## 🔍 Key Findings

1. Young users (20–30) dominate the platform
2. Clothing is the most popular category
3. Most users spend between ₹1000–₹5000
4. Customers prefer branded products over cheaper alternatives
5. Shopping frequency is moderate

---

## 📈 Business Impact

- Focus marketing on young users
- Promote clothing category
- Target mid-range pricing strategy
- Emphasize brand trust

---

## 🚀 Recommendations

- Offer discounts in ₹1000–₹5000 range
- Improve branding strategies
- Personalize recommendations
